About Me

I'm Tim Prescher, a systems-minded builder focused on, leadership, and innovation. I currently run organizations in the behavioral health and rehabilitation space, where our goal isn't just growth, it's meaningful transformation. I prioritize clarity, systems that work, and doing hard things the right way.

Most of my work today is about aligning people, process, and purpose. I've rebuilt legacy infrastructures, mentored leadership teams, and am actively exploring how technologies like AI and blockchain can make care more consistent and trauma-informed. Whether it's creating operational clarity or designing a new virtual care model, my goal is always the same: make the system serve people, not the other way around.

I don’t lead from theory. I lead from experience and foundations that were built under pressure while serving as an Infantryman in the United States Army. I've brought that same focus into every room I step into. This portfolio is a living expression of what I believe: Courage isn't loud, Systems should be built for people, and the best way forward is one built on trust to create lasting impact.
